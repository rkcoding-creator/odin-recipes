Pizza recipe

Stretched dough topped with tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, and basil, then baked at high heat until the crust is crisp and lightly charred at the edges.

Ingredients

    • flour
      active dry yeast
      war water
      olive oil
      salt
      crushed san marzano tomatoes
      fresh mozzarella
      fresh basil leaves
      garlic

    • Steps

    • Make dough: Dissolve 0.3 ounces active dry yeast in 1.3 cups warm water. Mix with 17.9 ounces 00 flour, 1 teaspoons salt, and 2 tablespoons olive oil. Knead 60 minutes , then let rise 1 hour.
      Make sauce: Mix 14.3 ounces crushed tomatoes with 1 pieces garlic clove (grated), a pinch of 1 teaspoons salt, and a drizzle of olive oil.
      Preheat: Heat oven to maximum (250–280°C) with a baking stone inside for 30 minutes
      Shape & top: Stretch dough into rounds. Spread sauce, tear over 8.9 ounces fresh mozzarella.
      Bake: Bake 8–10 minutes, until crust is puffed and charred. Top with 10 pieces fresh basil.
