Couscous recipe

Steamed semolina grains served with a fragrant stew of slow-cooked vegetables or lamb, seasoned with cumin, coriander, and a pinch of harissa.

Ingredients

    • semolina
      chickpeas
      zucchini,carrots and turnips
      onion
      vegetable broth
      cumin and coriander
      harissa paste
      olive and salt
      fresh cilantro

    • Steps

    • Cook stew: Sauté 1 pieces onion in oil. Add 1 teaspoons cumin & coriander, then 2 pieces carrot, 1 pieces turnip, and 3.1 cups vegetable broth. Simmer 25 minutes . Add 1 pieces zucchini, 14.3 ounces chickpeas, drained, and 1 tablespoons harissa paste. Cook 15 more min.
      Steam couscous: Pour boiling salted water over 10.7 ounces couscous (equal volume). Cover and steam 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork.
      Serve: Spoon stew over couscous. Garnish with 1 pieces fresh cilantro.
